Project Managment

Project management is the practice of using specific skills, tools, and techniques to plan, organize, and execute a project to achieve a defined goal, ensuring it meets specific requirements within a set timeframe and budget; essentially, it's the process of identifying a problem, creating a plan to solve it, and then carrying out that plan to completion.

Principle of Project Managment

1. Planning

2. Organizing

3. Securing

4. Managing

5. Leading

6. Controlling resources to achieve specific goals

Five stages of project Managment

1. Initiation

2. planning

3. execution

4. monitoring

5. closure

Benefits of Project Management

A good Project management tools offers several benefits, including improved planning and organization, efficient resource allocation, better communication and collaboration, risk mitigation, cost control, on-time delivery, enhanced quality, increased productivity, better decision-making, and improved customer satisfaction. It essentially ensures projects are completed within budget, timeframe, and desired quality standards.
